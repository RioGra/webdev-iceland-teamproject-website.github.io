Icelandic Horses

Icelandic Horses are the only breed of horse in Iceland. They have been bred there for over 1000 years, making them one of the purest breeds in the world. They have Wonderful Personalities.

STROKKUR GEYSER

Strokkur geyser in Southwestern Iceland erupts every 8-10 minutes. Since their discovery, geysers have fascinated mankind. As a fairly rare sight that can only usually be found in active volcanic areas. Iceland is a geyser hotspot with over 50 active and dormant geysers.
